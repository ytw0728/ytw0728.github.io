Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TL;DR: Slash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

Overview

Slash is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how Slash handles your data and information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

Slash operates entirely on your local device. We do not:

  • Collect or store your bookmarks
  • Track your browsing history
  • Gather analytics or usage data
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Share any information with third parties

Local Storage

Slash stores the following information locally on your device:

Chrome Extension

  • Settings: Your preferences such as language, keyboard shortcuts, and root bookmark folder selection
  • Favorite bookmarks: Paths to bookmarks you've marked as favorites

Desktop Application

  • Configuration: Chrome profile selection, language preference, and application settings
  • Auto-launch preference: Whether the app should start automatically

CLI Tool

  • Configuration: Chrome profile selection and default settings

All this data is stored locally using:

  • Chrome Extension: Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local)
  • Desktop App: Local configuration files in your system's application data directory
  • CLI: Configuration files in your home directory

Bookmark Access

Slash reads your Chrome bookmarks directly from your local Chrome profile. This access is:

  • Read-only (Slash does not modify your bookmarks)
  • Local-only (bookmarks never leave your device)
  • On-demand (only accessed when you use the application)

Permissions

Slash requires the following permissions:

Chrome Extension

  • bookmarks: To read and search your bookmarks
  • storage: To save your settings locally
  • tabs: To open bookmarks in new tabs

Desktop Application

  • File system access: To read Chrome bookmarks from your local Chrome profile directory
  • System tray: To provide quick access via system tray icon

These permissions are used solely for the functionality described and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

Slash does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Updates

Application updates are delivered through:

  • Chrome Extension: Chrome Web Store (managed by Google)
  • Desktop App: GitHub Releases or direct download
  • CLI: Package managers or direct download

We do not track who downloads or updates the application.

Children's Privacy

Slash does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect any contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please:

  • Contact us via email: [your-email@example.com]

Your Rights

Since Slash does not collect any personal data:

  • There is no data to access, modify, or delete on our end
  • You have complete control over all data stored locally on your device
  • You can uninstall Slash at any time to remove all local data

Summary: Slash is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your bookmarks and settings never leave your device. We cannot access your data because we never receive it in the first place.
